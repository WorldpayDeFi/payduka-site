Every card swipe on a PayDuka PoS triggers instant on-chain settlement — merchants get paid immediately, fees are reimbursed, and PDuka supply deflates with every transaction.

// Monetary Policy

A Transparent, Self-Sustaining Token Economy

Designed to power African commerce — forever. Fixed supply, perpetual burns, circular treasury.

21BTotal Supply
NeverNew Minting
PerpetualBurn Model
PolygonNetwork

Fixed Supply — No Exceptions

PDuka operates on an absolute fixed supply of 21,000,000,000 tokens — encoded directly into the smart contract at genesis. No new PDuka will ever be created. As merchant adoption increases, the same fixed pool powers more and more economic activity.

The 3-Way Split

Every payment processed through a PayDuka PoS terminal triggers an automated on-chain settlement. The allocated PDuka is divided automatically:

2%
0.5%
97.5%
2% Treasury Return

Returns to PayDuka's protocol treasury. Continuously refills the reserve used for future merchant settlements.

0.5% Permanent Burn 🔥

Sent to the burn address and removed from the 21B supply forever. Every transaction makes PDuka scarcer.

97.5% Merchant Settlement (ZARP)

Converts to ZARP — a ZAR-pegged stablecoin — and settles instantly into the merchant's wallet. Zero volatility risk.

⚖️ PayDuka's Golden Rule

Skim Rate > Burn Rate. Treasury receives 2% every transaction, only 0.5% is burned. The treasury nets +1.5% per transaction — growing stronger with every payment processed.
