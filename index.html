// How It Works

Card Payment Flow Reimagined for Africa

Every card swipe on a PayDuka PoS triggers instant on-chain settlement — merchants get paid immediately, fees are reimbursed, and PDuka supply deflates with every transaction.

STEP 01
💳

Customer Taps or Swipes

The merchant's PayDuka terminal accepts the payment. The PoS broadcasts a signed transaction to Polygon in under 2 seconds.

sub-2s response
STEP 02
🔗

On-Chain Settlement

Polygon confirms the block. The stablecoin (USDC/USDT) is transferred directly to the merchant wallet — no bank, no delays.

gas < $0.01
STEP 03
🔥

PDuka Burn & Reward

0.1% of each transaction burns PDuka tokens, reducing supply. Both merchant and customer earn PDuka loyalty rewards automatically.

deflationary
STEP 04
📊

Real-Time Dashboard

Merchants see live revenue, PDuka earned, and transaction history. Export to any accounting software. Full on-chain audit trail.

fully transparent
STEP 05
🌍

Ecosystem Growth

PDuka earned can be staked for APY, used in Play-to-Earn games, or held for governance. Every transaction strengthens the entire ecosystem.

8–15% staking · P2E games
